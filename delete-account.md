# Account Deletion

**Effective date:** 2026-06-10  
**App:** Stamp Collector  
**Operator:** Withawat Chotprom, Denmark  
**Contact:** withawat.chotprom@gmail.com

There are two ways to delete your Stamp Collector account and all data associated with it.

---

## Option 1 — In the app (instant)

1. Open Stamp Collector on your device.
2. Go to **Settings → Account**.
3. Tap **Delete my account**.
4. Confirm.

Your account and all server-side data are deleted immediately. This is the fastest and recommended method.

## Option 2 — By email (if you no longer have the app)

If you have uninstalled the app or can't sign in, send an email to:

**withawat.chotprom@gmail.com**

Include the email address you used to register the account. We will verify your identity, process the deletion, and confirm within 30 days.

---

## What is deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is removed from our servers:

- Your account login (email address, password hash)
- Your profile record
- Your scan-quota record (monthly usage counter)
- Your cloud-backed stamp collection (Collector Plus subscribers only)
- Stamp photos stored in cloud backup (Collector Plus subscribers only)

## What is NOT deleted

- **Local data on your device.** Your collection stored locally on your phone is not deleted by this action. To remove it, uninstall the app, or use Settings → Clear local collection.
- **Subscription history with Google Play.** Cancellation of an active subscription is handled separately through your Google Play account. Account deletion in our system does not cancel your Play subscription — see our [Terms of Service](terms-of-service) for instructions on cancelling.

## Data retention after deletion

After deletion is confirmed, data is removed from active systems immediately. Encrypted database backups may retain a residual copy for up to **90 days** before being purged from the backup rotation, after which no copy remains anywhere.
